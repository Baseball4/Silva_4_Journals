Paid License - license requiring pay.

Shareware - free software distributed informally, after evaluation may request a fee,
to continue.

Trialware - free software for a period of time till fee is requested.

Ad-ware - program used but ad's added for purposes of preventing payment.

Crippleware - using only some of the program.

Freemium - pay to finish/go longer/win.
